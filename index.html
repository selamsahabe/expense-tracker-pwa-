Your Financial Questions Answered

Clear guidance to help you make the most of your financial journey

How do I install the Expense Tracker app?

You can install the app by clicking the "Add to Home Screen" option in your browser. The app includes a manifest file that ensures it works like a native app on your device.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! The app uses Service Workers and Cache API to enable offline access. You can log expenses and income even without an internet connection, and your data will sync automatically when you're back online.

Does the app use any native device features?

Yes! The app integrates geolocation to tag expenses with location data (optional) and push notifications to remind you of upcoming bills or budget limits.

How do I navigate between sections?

The app has three main views: Home, About, and Features. You can navigate between them using the menu in the header or footer.

Is my financial data secure?

We use bank-grade encryption to protect your data. Your information is stored locally on your device and never shared without your consent. Read our security policy.

What if I need help with the app?

You can reach out to our support team anytime through the contact page. We’re here to help you with any questions or issues you may have.
